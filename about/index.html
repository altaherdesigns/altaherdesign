Our Story

Forty years.
One standard.

From a single carpentry workshop in Fujairah in 1984 to two full divisions serving contractors and homeowners across all seven emirates. Still family-run. Still in Al Hail Industrial.

01 — History

How we got here.

A few moments that shaped what Al Taher Group is today — built slowly, step by step, in Fujairah.

1984

Founded in Fujairah

Al Taher Group begins as a small carpentry and joinery workshop in Al Hail Industrial Area, Fujairah — taking on villa fit-out work across the emirate.

1990s

Expanding into aluminium & glass

As Fujairah's construction sector grew through the nineties, we added aluminium windows, doors and glass works to our capabilities, serving developers building the new residential districts.

2000s

Metal fabrication becomes a specialism

Growing demand for custom gates, railings and architectural metalwork prompted us to formalise the metal fabrication side — adding certified welders, structural steel capability and eventually our own powder coating line.

2010s

Two divisions, one group

The business formally structured into two complementary divisions: Al Taher Carpentry, Aluminium & Metal Forming LLC for joinery, kitchens and glass; DAT Metal Fabrication & Powder Coating for gates, fabrication and finishing. Both remain under Al Taher Group ownership.

Today

40+ years, all 7 emirates

A trusted trade partner and direct supplier to homeowners, villa developers and contractors across the UAE — still headquartered in the same industrial area we started in, still doing every job in-house.
