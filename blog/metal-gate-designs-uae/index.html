Custom Metal Gate Designs
for UAE Villas — 2026 Guide

Sliding or swing? Mild steel or aluminium? Geometric or arabesque? Everything you need to know before ordering a custom villa gate in the UAE — from design styles to materials to what questions to ask your fabricator.

Your villa gate is the first thing anyone sees. It frames the entire property — and in the UAE, where villa facades are taken seriously, a poorly chosen gate can undermine an otherwise excellent home. A well-designed one, on the other hand, adds genuine kerb appeal and real resale value.

This guide covers everything you need to know before placing an order. We've been fabricating custom metal gates in Fujairah since 1984 and have installed hundreds across all seven emirates.

Step 1: Swing Gate or Sliding Gate?

This is the first decision, and it's mostly determined by your driveway layout rather than aesthetics.

Swing gates open like a door — either inward or outward. They look traditional, work well for narrower driveways, and are generally less expensive to fabricate and motorise. The catch: you need clearance space for the arc. If your driveway slopes upward toward the road, an inward-opening swing gate may scrape the ground — a very common problem in UAE villas.

Sliding gates roll horizontally along a track parallel to your boundary wall. They need no swing clearance, work on any driveway slope, and are the right choice if you have a wide entrance. They cost slightly more and require a longer wall section for the gate to slide against when open.

"If you're not sure, measure your driveway width and the wall length on the hinge side. Send us a photo — we can tell you in 60 seconds which type works for your plot."

A third option gaining popularity in UAE villas is the bi-fold gate, which folds in half as it opens. It combines the speed of sliding with a compact footprint — ideal for narrow plots in developments where boundary walls are tight.

For most UAE villa gates, mild steel with powder coating is the most popular combination — it's strong, cost-effective, takes any colour, and with proper powder coating will last 15–20 years without significant corrosion. If you're in a coastal area like Fujairah, Ras Al Khaimah, or waterfront Dubai, consider aluminium for the main frame — the salt air is particularly aggressive on steel even with good coating.

Step 3: Design Styles

The most common styles we fabricate for UAE villas:

Modern Geometric

Clean horizontal or vertical bars, minimal decoration, often with a laser-cut panel as a feature. These look excellent on contemporary villas and photograph well. Very popular in Dubai and Abu Dhabi developments from 2018 onward.

Arabic / Arabesque

Intricate geometric or floral patterns referencing Islamic architecture. These take more fabrication time (and cost more) but are genuinely distinctive. Often combined with a laser-cut overlay panel on a solid or bar-grid backing.

Classic / Colonial

Vertical bars with spear tips or finials, horizontal rails, optional decorative scrolls at corners. Timeless, suits older-style villas, and is generally the most cost-effective to fabricate as the shapes are standard.

Contemporary Cladded

A steel frame clad with timber-effect aluminium composite panels or stainless-steel sheets. Gives a solid, private appearance — no visibility through the gate. Very popular for corner plots and villas on busy roads where the owners want more privacy.

Step 4: Motorisation

Most clients motorise their gates — especially main vehicle gates. The motor adds AED 800–2,500 to the cost depending on gate weight and brand. Standard features on modern motors include:

  • Remote control (usually 2 fobs supplied)
  • Manual override (essential for power cuts)
  • Safety sensors to stop/reverse if something's in the way
  • GSM module (optional, allows phone-operated opening)

For sliding gates on heavy steel frames, specify a rack-and-pinion motor rather than a friction wheel — it's more reliable on dusty surfaces.

Step 5: Finish and Colour

Powder coating is the standard finish for steel gates in the UAE. The most popular colours are:

  • Matte black (RAL 9005) — the most requested finish in 2024–2026
  • Anthracite grey (RAL 7016) — softer alternative to black
  • Champagne / bronze (RAL 1019 or 1013) — complements beige/stone villas
  • White (RAL 9003) — traditional, suits white or off-white villas
  • Dark green (RAL 6009) — trending since 2023, works well with landscaping

For timber-effect finishes, specify a woodgrain texture coat — these are applied in the same process as standard powder coat and don't flake or crack in UV the way vinyl wraps do.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does fabrication take?

Typically 7–21 days depending on complexity and current workshop load. Simple bar-grid gates are faster; laser-cut arabesque panels take longer. We confirm the timeline at quoting stage.

Do you install across the UAE?

Yes. We fabricate in Fujairah and install across all seven emirates. Delivery and installation cost is included in the quote based on your location.

Can I see samples before ordering?

Visit our workshop in Al Hail Industrial Area, Fujairah — you can see material samples, finished gates and our powder coating range in person. Sat–Thu, 6am–8pm.

What if I already have a design?

Perfect — send us the drawing or even a rough sketch on paper. We'll convert it to shop drawings and confirm the price before anything is cut.
