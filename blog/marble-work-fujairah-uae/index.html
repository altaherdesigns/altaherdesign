Marble Work in Fujairah & the UAE
Floors, Staircases & Finishes

Marble is the finish UAE villas are judged by — and the trade where material choice, workmanship and coordination with metalwork decide whether it looks like luxury or liability. Here is how to choose stone, where marble belongs, and why marble-and-metal staircases reward a single workshop.

Marble is the finish UAE villas are judged by — floors, staircases, countertops and feature walls — and the result depends less on the stone's price tag than on selection, workmanship and coordination. The right stone in the right room, templated and installed properly, outlasts everything else in the house. Al Taher Group supplies and installs marble flooring, staircases, countertops and cladding from our Fujairah workshop — with the metal and joinery trades that marble usually meets, under the same roof.

Key takeaways

  • Match stone hardness to traffic: harder stones for floors, decorative stones for features.
  • Outdoors: honed or flamed finishes for grip; high polish belongs indoors.

Where does marble belong in a UAE villa?

Marble earns its place in entrances, living areas, staircases, bathrooms and kitchen worktops — each with a different specification. Entrance and living floors want hard, dense stone that shrugs off grit; bathrooms favour lighter stones with honed finishes for grip; kitchens want worktop stones sealed against staining. Getting the specification right per room is most of the craft.

How do you choose the right marble?

Three questions decide it: how hard is the stone, how is the room used, and how will the surface be finished? Polished surfaces give the classic mirror sheen and suit interiors; honed gives a soft matte that hides wear; flamed gives outdoor grip. Colour and veining are taste — hardness and finish are engineering. We walk clients through physical samples against their light and layout before a single slab is cut.

Marble-and-metal staircases: the case for one workshop

The signature staircase of modern UAE villas — marble treads on a steel structure with glass or metal balustrades — is really two trades pretending to be one. The stone must be templated to the metal, the balustrade fixings must land where the stone allows, and the tolerances of both must agree. When the marble team and the metal fabrication team share a workshop, that conversation happens at the bench instead of between two subcontractors' schedules. That is the practical advantage of doing railings, fabrication and marble in-house — one template, one schedule, one name responsible.

Countertops, cladding and feature work

Kitchen and vanity countertops are templated after carcasses are installed, cut with sink and hob openings in the workshop, and edge-profiled to your design. Wall cladding and feature panels — bookmatched slabs, reception walls, fireplace surrounds — follow the same rule: measure the real structure, cut to it, install once.

What drives marble cost in the UAE?

Stone choice first, everything else second. The spread between a dependable beige marble and an imported statement stone is severalfold per square metre before labour. After stone: surface finish, edge profiles, stair details like bullnose nosings, and site complexity. The way to keep a marble budget honest is an itemised quote per area — which is how we price it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which marble is best for flooring in the UAE?

For high-traffic floors, harder and denser stones handle grit and foot traffic better and keep their polish longer. Lighter marbles brighten interiors and stay cooler underfoot, which suits the climate; softer decorative stones are better kept to feature walls and low-traffic areas. The honest answer is per-room: we match stone hardness and finish to how each space is used.

Is marble a good idea outdoors in Fujairah?

With the right stone and finish, yes. Outdoors we recommend flamed or honed (non-polished) finishes for grip, and denser stones that tolerate heat and salt air. High-polish marble outdoors becomes slippery when wet and dulls faster in direct sun.

What is a marble-and-metal staircase?

A staircase with marble treads and risers carried or trimmed by metalwork — a steel stringer or frame, with stainless, aluminium or glass balustrades. It pairs the solidity of stone with the slim lines of metal. The catch is coordination: the stone and the metal must be templated to each other, which is why a workshop that does both in-house delivers a cleaner result.

Do you install marble countertops for kitchens?

Yes. We template after cabinets are set, cut and edge the stone to the actual carcass dimensions, and install with the sink, hob and drainage cut-outs done in the workshop rather than improvised on site.

What does marble work cost in the UAE?

Stone choice moves the price more than anything else — the spread between a workhorse beige marble and an imported statement stone is severalfold per square metre. After the stone itself, expect cost drivers in surface finish (polished, honed, flamed), edge profiles, stair nosing details and installation complexity.
