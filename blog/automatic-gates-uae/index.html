Automatic Gates in the UAE:
Sliding, Swing & Smart Access

An automatic gate trades a daily chore for a button press — and adds real security and kerb appeal. Here is how sliding and swing automation works, the ways to open a gate, the safety features that matter in UAE conditions, and what to budget.

An automatic gate is a sliding or swing gate fitted with an electric motor and a control system, so it opens and closes by remote, keypad, intercom or phone rather than by hand. In the UAE they are the standard choice for villa entrances and compound gates: they add security and privacy, and they spare you stepping out into 45°C heat to push a gate. At Al Taher Group we fabricate and install automatic main gates across all seven emirates, matching the right motor to the gate and the driveway.

This guide covers how automatic gates work, the difference between sliding and swing automation, how you open them, and the safety features worth insisting on in a UAE home.

What makes a gate "automatic"?

Four parts turn an ordinary gate into an automated one:

  • The gate leaf — the gate itself, in cast aluminium, steel or wrought iron. Cast aluminium is the popular choice for automated villa gates because it is light (kinder to the motor) and will not rust in coastal humidity.
  • The operator (motor) — a sliding-gate motor that drives a toothed rack along the gate, or swing arms/rams that push each leaf open.
  • The control board — the "brain" that runs the motor and talks to the remotes and sensors.
  • Access and safety devices — remote fobs, a keypad or intercom, and photocell safety beams.

Sliding or swing? It usually comes down to the driveway

The first decision is the gate type, and your space and slope decide it more than taste:

Sliding gateSwing gate
Best whenDriveway is short or sloped; you want to park close to the gateYou have room for the leaves to swing inward and the ground is level
Space neededClear run along the wall for the gate to slide intoClear arc inside the gate for the leaves to open
SlopeHandles a sloped driveway wellStruggles on a slope unless the ground is levelled
Wide openingsGood for very wide entrancesBetter as a double (two-leaf) gate for wide spans

How do you open an automatic gate?

Modern operators give you several ways in, and you can combine them:

  • Remote fob — the everyday method from inside the car.
  • Keypad — a code on the wall for family or staff without a remote.
  • Video intercom — see and speak to visitors, then release the gate from inside the house.
  • Smartphone app — open the gate and get alerts from your phone, anywhere.
  • Loop detector — a sensor in the driveway that opens the gate automatically as your car approaches from inside.

Safety features worth insisting on

An automated gate is heavy and moving, so the safety devices are not optional extras:

  • Photocell beams — if anything crosses the opening while the gate is closing, it stops and reverses. Essential where children or pets are around.
  • Soft start and stop — the gate eases into motion and slows before stopping, reducing wear and shock.
  • Manual release — a key release so you can open the gate by hand during a power cut.
  • Battery backup — keeps the gate working through the short power interruptions that do happen.
"The motor is only as good as the gate it is fitted to. A well-built, properly balanced gate makes the automation reliable; a heavy, poorly hung gate burns out operators. We build the gate and fit the motor as one job, so the two are matched."

What do automatic gates cost in the UAE?

Price depends on the gate (size, material and design), the operator brand, and the access and safety kit you choose. As a rough guide, automation adds a motor-and-controls package on top of the gate itself, and a video intercom or app control adds a little more. Because every entrance is different — width, slope, power supply and finish all matter — the only accurate figure is a written quote after a quick site look. Frequently Asked Questions

Do you build the gate and fit the automation, or just supply the motor?

We do both. We fabricate the gate in cast aluminium, steel or wrought iron in our Fujairah workshop, then fit and program the automation, sensors and access controls as one job — so the motor is correctly matched to the weight and size of the gate.

What happens to an automatic gate during a power cut?

Every automated gate we install includes a manual key release so you can open it by hand, and we recommend a battery backup so the gate keeps working through short power interruptions common in some areas.

Can I open my gate from my phone?

Yes. Most modern operators support a smartphone app for opening the gate and receiving alerts, and can be paired with a video intercom so you can see and speak to visitors before letting them in.
