Glass Works in Fujairah
Partitions, Shower Enclosures & Balustrades

Frameless partitions, shower enclosures, balustrades and skylights all look simple — until glass spec, fittings and UAE safety rules enter the picture. Here is what each application needs, what drives cost, and how in-house measurement and installation changes the result.

Glass works in the UAE come down to four applications — partitions, shower enclosures, balustrades and skylights — and each has its own glass spec, fittings and safety rules. Get those right and glass is one of the most durable finishes in the house; get them wrong and you see leaking enclosures, corroded fittings and glass that should never have been used where it was. Al Taher Group measures, supplies and installs all four from our Fujairah workshop, serving all seven emirates.

Key takeaways

  • Showers and balustrades legally need safety glass — toughened, or toughened-laminated.
  • Frameless looks lighter but needs thicker glass (10–12mm) and better hardware.
  • On the coast, the fittings — not the glass — are what corrode: specify stainless or powder-coated.
  • Custom templating after tiling is what makes enclosures watertight.
  • One team measuring, cutting and installing removes the usual glazier-vs-fitter blame game.

Glass partitions: dividing space without losing light

Partitions are the workhorse of office and villa glass. Framed aluminium-glass systems give structure, acoustic seals and budget control; frameless systems give the clean, all-glass look for reception areas and living spaces. The full framed-vs-frameless decision — including acoustics and ceiling heights — is covered in our dedicated aluminium glass partition guide.

Shower enclosures: where precision decides everything

A shower enclosure is only as good as its template. Walls are rarely perfectly plumb and tiled corners are rarely square, so a quality enclosure is measured after tiling, cut to the real opening, and edged and drilled for the exact hinge positions. We fabricate walk-in screens, hinged-door and sliding enclosures in toughened glass with the seals and channels matched to your layout. The result is a door that swings true and an enclosure that keeps water where it belongs.

Glass balustrades: safety glass doing structural work

On stairs, mezzanines and balconies, glass stops being decoration and becomes a barrier. That is why balustrade glass is toughened or toughened-laminated, thicker than partition glass, and fixed in structural channels, standoff pins or clamps rated for the load. Outdoors in Fujairah, the specification that matters most is the hardware: salt air destroys cheap fittings long before it touches the glass, so we fit corrosion-resistant stainless or powder-coated aluminium channels. For costs across railing materials, see our stair railing cost guide.

Skylights and feature glazing

Roof glazing brings daylight deep into a villa — and it must carry heat, load and safety requirements at once. We fabricate framed skylights and decorative laser-cut screens over glass, using laminated safety glass overhead as standard.

Why in-house measurement and installation matters

Most glass problems are not glass problems — they are coordination problems. A glazier cuts to sizes someone else measured; an installer fits panels someone else cut. When measurement, cutting, edging and installation run through one team, tolerances stay under one roof and there is one name responsible for the result. That is how we have run glass works at Al Taher since 1984.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between framed and frameless glass?

Framed systems hold the glass in aluminium profiles — stronger seals, better acoustics, lower cost. Frameless systems use thicker toughened glass with minimal patch fittings or channels for an all-glass look. Frameless needs heavier glass (typically 10–12mm) precisely because there is no frame doing structural work.

What glass is required for showers and balustrades in the UAE?

Safety glass. Shower enclosures use toughened (tempered) glass, which crumbles into blunt granules instead of shards if broken. Balustrades use toughened or toughened-laminated glass depending on the design and whether there is a continuous handrail. Ordinary annealed glass is not acceptable for either.

Can you make a shower enclosure to a custom size?

Yes — most of our shower enclosures are custom. We template the space after the tiling is finished, cut and edge the glass to the actual opening, and install with the hinges, seals and hardware suited to your layout: walk-in screens, hinged doors or sliding enclosures.

Do glass balustrades work outdoors in Fujairah's climate?

Yes, with the right specification. The glass itself is unaffected by salt air; the fittings are the critical part. We use corrosion-resistant stainless fittings or powder-coated aluminium channels so the hardware lasts as long as the glass on coastal balconies and terraces.

How much do glass works cost in the UAE?

Glass pricing follows thickness, treatment and hardware: toughened costs more than annealed, laminated more again, and frameless hardware more than framed profiles. A framed partition is the budget path; a frameless walk-in shower or structural balustrade sits higher. Send sizes for an itemised quote — glass is measured and priced per opening.
