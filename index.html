03 — Our Story

Built in Fujairah,
working across the UAE.

Al Taher Group started as a single carpentry workshop in 1984. Over four decades, we've grown into two complementary divisions — one for carpentry, aluminium and glass, the other for metal fabrication and powder coating — operating under one name from Al Hail Industrial Area, Fujairah.

"Everything we deliver leaves our own workshop — measured, welded, finished and installed by our team."

Today we serve homeowners building villas across the emirates and contractors who need a reliable trade partner. We don't sub out the work. We do it.
