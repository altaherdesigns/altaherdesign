About Al Taher Group

Four Decades of Gulf Craftsmanship

Founded in 1984 in Fujairah, Al Taher Group has grown from a carpentry workshop into a full-service industrial group. We serve both homeowners looking to transform their villas and contractors who need reliable execution on large-scale projects.

Our two divisions — Al Taher Carpentry, Aluminium & Metal Forming LLC and DAT Metal Fabrication & Powder Coating — give clients complete capability under one trusted name. Every job is handled in-house from our Al Hail Industrial workshop.

Whether you want a custom gate, a new kitchen, a glass staircase railing or a pergola for your outdoor space — we handle it all. We work directly with homeowners, visit your site, and deliver exactly what you envision.

  • Kitchen cabinets built and installed to your specifications
  • Stair railings in aluminium, iron, glass or a combination
