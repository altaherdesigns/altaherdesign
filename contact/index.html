03 — FAQ

Questions we
hear often.

Do you serve areas outside Fujairah?

Yes — we serve all 7 UAE emirates. Our workshop is in Al Hail Industrial Area, Fujairah, but we deliver and install across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ras Al Khaimah, Ajman and Umm Al Quwain. Travel and installation costs outside Fujairah are included in your quote.

How much does a custom metal gate cost in the UAE?

Cost depends on size, material (mild steel, stainless or aluminium), design complexity and powder-coating colour. We provide free, detailed written quotes. The fastest way to get a price is to send dimensions or a photo via WhatsApp — we typically respond within a few hours.

What is powder coating and why does it matter in the UAE?

Powder coating is a dry finishing process: electrostatically charged powder is applied to metal then cured under heat. It produces a hard, even finish that resists chipping, fading, corrosion and UV — significantly better than wet paint in UAE coastal heat and salt air. Our DAT division runs its own in-house powder coating line with every RAL colour available, so finish quality stays under our control.

How long does a pergola installation take?

Most standard pergola installations take 3–7 working days from delivery to completion. A firm timeline is agreed at quote stage after our site survey. Larger or more complex structures may take longer — we'll always be clear upfront.

Do you work with contractors and developers?

Yes, contractor and developer work is a significant part of what we do. We offer trade pricing on volume orders, credit terms for established contractors, a dedicated project manager as a single point of contact, and coordinated delivery schedules to fit your programme. Contact our General Manager on +971 50 670 5015 to discuss arrangements.

What makes Al Taher Group different?

Three things: over 40 years of continuous operation since 1984; 100% in-house work — we fabricate, weld, powder-coat and install everything using our own team with nothing sub-contracted; and two complementary divisions (carpentry, aluminium & glass on one side; metal fabrication & powder coating on the other) that together can cover a complete villa fit-out under a single contract.
